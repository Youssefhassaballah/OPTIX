Setup & Care Instructions

Getting Started with Your New Loupes

It's normal for new users to experience slight discomfort or dizziness at first, especially when transitioning to higher magnification levels. A 2–3 week adjustment period is recommended. Start by wearing your loupes for no more than one hour per day, and gradually increase usage as your hand-eye coordination improves.

⸻

Indications for Use

Loupes are precision optical devices designed to magnify oral cavities and surgical fields. They are intended for use only by qualified medical and dental professionals.

⸻

Cleaning & Disinfecting

To ensure long-lasting performance, follow these care instructions:

Avoid the following:

  • Spraying liquids directly on the device
  • Rinsing or submerging in water or ultrasonic cleaners
  • Using autoclaves, alcohol over 70%, or harsh disinfectants (e.g., glutaraldehyde, iodophor)
  • Cleaning lenses with paper towels
