Cleaning & Maintenance Protocol

Proper care ensures optical clarity and extends product lifespan. Follow these evidence-based cleaning procedures:

Prohibited Cleaning Methods
  • Direct liquid application: Never spray liquids directly on optical surfaces
  • Immersion cleaning: Avoid rinsing or submerging in any liquids, including water and ultrasonic cleaners
  • Harsh disinfectants: Excludes alcohol concentrations >70%, glutaraldehyde, or iodophor solutions
  • Abrasive materials: Paper towels or other non-lens approved cleaning materials
  • Heat sterilization: Autoclaving will permanently damage optical components

Recommended Cleaning Procedure

  1. Use only manufacturer-provided or approved lens cleaning solutions
  2. Apply solution to microfiber cloth, not directly to lenses
  3. Gently wipe in circular motions from center outward
  4. Store in protective case when not in use
