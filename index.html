WHO ARE WE?

OptiX is a brand specialized in Dental Loupes, high-precision magnification eyewear used by dentists. The brand aims to provide premium-quality tools that enhance Visibility, Accuracy and Professionalism in dental work.

KEY FEATURES

Lightweight & Comfortable

Designed for extended use without discomfort with optimal weight distribution

High-Precision Optics

Premium-quality Germany Schott optical lenses ensure superior magnification and crystal-clear visibility

Enhanced Illumination

Built-in LED light source with 65,000 lux intensity and 5500K daylight color temperature

Engineered for Dental Professionals

  • Multiple magnification options (2.5X – 3.5X – 4X – 5X – 6X – 8X)
  • Adjustable brightness & focus for customized experience
  • Anti-fog lenses maintain clear vision in all conditions
  • Medium-size design perfect for daily dental procedures
  • Long battery life with 5-6 hours continuous use
